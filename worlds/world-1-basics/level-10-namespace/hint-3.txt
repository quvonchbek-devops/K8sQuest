💡 Yechimga yaqin maslahat:

Tuzatish qadamlari:
1. default namespace dan o'chiring: `kubectl delete -f broken.yaml`
2. broken.yaml dagi BARCHA `namespace: default` ni `namespace: k8squest` ga o'zgartiring
3. To'g'ri namespace ga apply qiling: `kubectl apply -f broken.yaml`
4. Tekshiring: `kubectl get all -n k8squest`

Barcha resurslar endi k8squest namespace da bo'lishi kerak!

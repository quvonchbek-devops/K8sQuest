💡 Yechimga yaqin maslahat:

"nonexistent-tag-xyz-123" image tag mavjud emas — uni to'g'ri tag (`nginx:latest`) ga o'zgartirish kerak.

Bu sandbox terminalida faqat kubectl ishlaydi (fayl tahrirlagich yo'q), shuning uchun broken.yaml ni tahrirlash o'rniga jonli podning image'ini to'g'ridan-to'g'ri o'zgartiring. Podni o'chirish SHART EMAS:

    kubectl set image pod/web-app web=nginx:latest -n k8squest

Bu yerda `web` — konteyner nomi (describe chiqishida `spec.containers{web}` deb ko'ringan). kubelet yangi image ni tortadi va pod ishga tushadi.

Keyin tekshiring:

    kubectl get pod web-app -n k8squest

Muqobil yo'l — interaktiv tahrir: `kubectl edit pod web-app -n k8squest` bilan `image:` qatorini `nginx:latest` ga o'zgartirib saqlang.

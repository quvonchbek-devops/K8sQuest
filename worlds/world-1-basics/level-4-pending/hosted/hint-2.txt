🧭 Yo'naltiruvchi maslahat:

`kubectl apply -f broken.yaml` shunga o'xshash xato beradi:

  The Pod "hungry-app" is invalid: spec.containers[0].resources.requests:
  Invalid value: "999": must be less than or equal to cpu limit of 200m

Bu — LimitRange. U namespace da har bir container so'rashi mumkin bo'lgan
maksimumni belgilaydi.

Namespace ning chegaralarini ko'ring:

  kubectl describe limitrange
  kubectl describe quota

Pod nima so'rayotganini ko'ring:

  kubectl get -f broken.yaml -o jsonpath='{.spec.containers[0].resources}'

Ikkalasini solishtiring: pod qancha so'rayapti va ruxsat etilgani qancha?

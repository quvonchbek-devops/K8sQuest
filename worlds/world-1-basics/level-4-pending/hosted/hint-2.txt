🧭 Yo'naltiruvchi maslahat:

`kubectl apply -f broken.yaml` shunga o'xshash xato beradi:

  The Pod "hungry-app" is invalid: spec.containers[0].resources.requests:
  Invalid value: "999": must be less than or equal to cpu limit of 200m

Bu — LimitRange. Namespace ning resurs qoidalarini ko'ring:

  kubectl describe limitrange

Diqqat qiling: broken.yaml da faqat `requests` bor, `limits` YO'Q. Container
limit e'lon qilmasa, LimitRange unga o'zining "Default Limit" ini beradi
(cpu 200m). Kubernetes da esa qat'iy qoida bor:

  request  <=  limit

Ya'ni siz 999 CPU so'radingiz, lekin limitingiz — 200m. Shuning uchun rad
etildi.

Pod nima so'rayotganini ko'ring va ruxsat etilgan qiymatlar bilan
solishtiring.

🔍 Kuzatuv maslahat:

Avval pod ishlayotganini tekshiring: `kubectl get pod backend-app -n k8squest`

Keyin service ni tekshiring: `kubectl get service backend-service -n k8squest`

Nihoyat, service pod larni topganini tekshiring: `kubectl get endpoints backend-service -n k8squest`

Birorta endpoint (IP manzil) ko'ryapsizmi? Agar yo'q bo'lsa, service pod ga ulanmagan.

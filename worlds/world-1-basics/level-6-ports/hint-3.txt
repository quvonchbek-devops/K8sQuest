💡 Yechimga yaqin maslahat:

Tuzatish qadamlari:
1. Resurslarni o'chiring: `kubectl delete -f broken.yaml`
2. broken.yaml da service ning targetPort ini 8080 dan 80 ga o'zgartiring
3. Apply qiling: `kubectl apply -f broken.yaml`
4. Test (ixtiyoriy): `kubectl port-forward service/web-service 8080:80 -n k8squest`
   Keyin brauzerda http://localhost:8080 ga o'ting

💡 Yechimga yaqin maslahat:

Tuzatish qadamlari:
1. Pod ni o'chiring: `kubectl delete pod app-with-logging -n k8squest`
2. broken.yaml da log-sidecar buyrug'ini ishlaydigan biriga o'zgartiring:
   ```
   command: ["sh", "-c", "while true; do echo 'Logging...' >> /var/log/app.log; sleep 5; done"]
   ```
3. Apply qiling: `kubectl apply -f broken.yaml`
4. Ikkala konteyner ishlayotganini tekshiring: `kubectl get pod app-with-logging -n k8squest`

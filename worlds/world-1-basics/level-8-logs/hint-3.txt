💡 Yechimga yaqin maslahat:

Tuzatish qadamlari:
1. Pod ni o'chiring: `kubectl delete pod database-app -n k8squest`
2. broken.yaml ga environment variable qo'shing:
   ```yaml
   env:
   - name: POSTGRES_PASSWORD
     value: "mysecretpassword"
   ```
3. Apply qiling: `kubectl apply -f broken.yaml`
4. Tekshiring: `kubectl logs database-app -n k8squest`

"database system is ready to accept connections" xabarini ko'rishingiz kerak!

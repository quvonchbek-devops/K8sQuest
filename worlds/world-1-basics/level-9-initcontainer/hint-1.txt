🔍 Kuzatuv maslahat:

Pod holatini tekshiring: `kubectl get pod web-with-init -n k8squest`

STATUS ustunida "Init:0/1" ko'rinadi, ya'ni init container hali tugamagan.

`kubectl describe pod web-with-init -n k8squest` buyrug'ini ishlating va Init Containers bo'limiga qarang.
Loglarni tekshiring: `kubectl logs web-with-init -c wait-for-service -n k8squest`

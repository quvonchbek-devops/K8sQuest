💡 Yechimga yaqin maslahat:

To'g'ri yechim — init container kutayotgan service ni yaratish!

Tuzatish qadamlari:
1. "backend-service" nomli service yarating:
   ```yaml
   apiVersion: v1
   kind: Service
   metadata:
     name: backend-service
     namespace: k8squest
   spec:
     selector:
       app: backend
     ports:
     - protocol: TCP
       port: 80
       targetPort: 8080
   ```

2. Service ni apply qiling: `kubectl apply -f <service-fayl-nomi.yaml>`

3. Service mavjud bo'lganda, init container ning nslookup buyrug'i muvaffaqiyatli bajariladi va tugaydi!

Service topilgandan keyin pod avtomatik ravishda asosiy konteynerga o'tadi.

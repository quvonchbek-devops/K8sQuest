🧭 Yo'naltiruvchi maslahat:

Deployment qotib qolgan, chunki yangi image mavjud emas. Tekshiring:

`kubectl describe deployment web-app -n k8squest`

Events bo'limiga qarang. ImagePullBackOff xatolarini ko'rasiz.

Rollout tarixini tekshiring:
`kubectl rollout history deployment/web-app -n k8squest`

Oldingi versiyaga rollback qilish mumkin!

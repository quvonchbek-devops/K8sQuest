🔍 Kuzatuv maslahat:

Pod larni tekshiring:
`kubectl get pods -n k8squest -l app=slow-app`

READY ustuniga e'tibor bering. Ba'zi pod lar STATUS "Running" bo'lsa ham "0/1" ko'rsatishi mumkin.

Service endpoint larini tekshiring:
`kubectl get endpoints slow-app-service -n k8squest`

Tayyor bo'lmagan pod lar service ga qo'shilayaptimi? Bu tayyor bo'lmagan pod larga trafik yuborilishiga olib keladi!

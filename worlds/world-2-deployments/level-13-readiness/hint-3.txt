💡 Yechimga yaqin maslahat:

Ilova haqiqatan tayyor bo'lguncha kutadigan readiness probe qo'shish kerak.

Qadamlar:
1. Deployment ni tahrirlang:
   `kubectl edit deployment slow-startup-app -n k8squest`

2. `livenessProbe` bo'limini toping va uning pastiga `readinessProbe` qo'shing:

```yaml
        readinessProbe:
          httpGet:
            path: /
            port: 80
          initialDelaySeconds: 22  # 20s sleep + 2s zaxira
          periodSeconds: 5
          successThreshold: 1
```

3. Saqlang va chiqing. Pod lar qayta yaratiladi.

4. Pod lar tayyor bo'lishini kuzating:
   `kubectl get pods -n k8squest -l app=slow-app -w`

Endi pod lar haqiqatan tayyor bo'lguncha trafik qabul qilmaydi!

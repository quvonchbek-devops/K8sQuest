💡 Yechimga yaqin maslahat:

kind/lokal klasterlar uchun metrics-server o'rnatish:

1. metrics-server manifestini apply qiling:
   ```bash
   kubectl apply -f https://github.com/kubernetes-sigs/metrics-server/releases/latest/download/components.yaml
   kubectl patch deployment metrics-server -n kube-system --type='json' -p='[{"op": "add", "path": "/spec/template/spec/containers/0/args/-", "value": "--kubelet-insecure-tls"}]'
   ```

2. metrics-server ishga tushishini kuting:
   `kubectl wait --for=condition=available --timeout=60s deployment/metrics-server -n kube-system`

3. HPA endi ishlashini tekshiring:
   `kubectl get hpa -n k8squest`

TARGETS endi haqiqiy CPU foizini ko'rsatishi kerak!

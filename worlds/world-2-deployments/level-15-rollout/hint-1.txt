🔍 Kuzatuv maslahat:

Deployment ning rollout strategiyasini tekshiring:
`kubectl get deployment critical-api -n k8squest -o yaml | grep -A 5 strategy`

`maxUnavailable` va `maxSurge` ga qarang. Nima sezayapsiz?

Rollout ni ishga tushirib ko'ring (image tag o'zgartiring):
`kubectl set image deployment/critical-api api=hashicorp/http-echo:latest -n k8squest`

Pod larga nima bo'lishini kuzating:
`kubectl get pods -n k8squest -l app=api -w`

BARCHA pod lar bir vaqtda to'xtaydimi? Bu to'xtash vaqtiga olib keladi!

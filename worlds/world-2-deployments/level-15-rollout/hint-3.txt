💡 Yechimga yaqin maslahat:

Nol to'xtashni ta'minlash uchun rollout strategiyasini tuzating.

Qadamlar:
1. Deployment ni tahrirlang:
   `kubectl edit deployment critical-api -n k8squest`

2. `strategy` bo'limini topib o'zgartiring:

```yaml
  strategy:
    type: RollingUpdate
    rollingUpdate:
      maxUnavailable: 1     # Bir vaqtda faqat 1 pod to'xtaydi
      maxSurge: 1           # Rollout vaqtida 1 ta qo'shimcha pod ga ruxsat
```

Bu ta'minlaydi:
- 3 replikada kamida 2 pod doim ishlaydi (3 - 1 = 2)
- Rollout vaqtida 4 tagacha pod bo'lishi mumkin (3 + 1 = 4)
- Nol to'xtash kafolatlangan!

3. Saqlang va tekshiring:
   `kubectl get deployment critical-api -n k8squest -o yaml | grep -A 5 strategy`

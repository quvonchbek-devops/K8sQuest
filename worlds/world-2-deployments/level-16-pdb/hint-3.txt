💡 Yechimga yaqin maslahat:

Mavjudlikni saqlab eviction larga ruxsat berish uchun PDB ni tuzating.

Qadamlar:
1. PDB ni tahrirlang:
   `kubectl edit pdb db-proxy-pdb -n k8squest`

2. minAvailable ni 3 dan 2 ga o'zgartiring:

```yaml
spec:
  minAvailable: 2  # 2 pod saqlang, 1 tasini evict qilishga ruxsat
```

3. Saqlang va tekshiring:
   `kubectl get pdb db-proxy-pdb -n k8squest`

Endi "ALLOWED" 0 o'rniga 1 bo'lishi kerak!

Muqobil: maxUnavailable ishlating:
```yaml
spec:
  maxUnavailable: 1  # 1 pod mavjud bo'lmasligiga ruxsat
```

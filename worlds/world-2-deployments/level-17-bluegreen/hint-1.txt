`kubectl get deployments -n k8squest`

app-blue va app-green ni ko'rishingiz kerak, ikkalasi ham 3/3 replika bilan.

`kubectl get service app-service -n k8squest -o yaml | grep -A 5 selector`

Qaysi versiyaga yo'naltirilgan?

Endpoint larni tekshiring:
`kubectl get endpoints app-service -n k8squest`

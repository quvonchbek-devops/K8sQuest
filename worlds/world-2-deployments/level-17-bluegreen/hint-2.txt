🧭 Yo'naltiruvchi maslahat:

Ikkita deployment ishlayapti:
- app-blue (eski versiya)
- app-green (yangi versiya)

Service selector:
```yaml
selector:
  app: myapp
  version: blue  # Muammo shu!
```

Bu service faqat IKKALA label ga mos pod larga trafik yo'naltiradi:
- app=myapp ✅
- version=blue ✅ (app-blue pod lariga mos)

app-green pod larda version=green bor, shuning uchun mos kelmaydi!

Foydalanuvchilar hali ham ESKI (blue) versiyani ko'rishmoqda.

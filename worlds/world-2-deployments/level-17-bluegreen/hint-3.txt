💡 Yechimga yaqin maslahat:

Blue-green deployment larda service selector ni yangilab trafik almashtirasiz.

Qadamlar:
1. Service ni tahrirlang:
   `kubectl edit service app-service -n k8squest`

2. Selector ni blue dan green ga o'zgartiring:

```yaml
spec:
  selector:
    app: myapp
    version: green  # blue dan green ga o'zgartirildi
```

3. Saqlang va chiqing.

4. Trafik endi green ga yo'naltirilganini tekshiring:
   `kubectl get endpoints app-service -n k8squest`

Green deployment dan 3 ta IP ko'rinishi kerak!

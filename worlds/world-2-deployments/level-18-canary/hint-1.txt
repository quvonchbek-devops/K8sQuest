🔍 Kuzatuv maslahat:

Deployment larni tekshiring:
`kubectl get deployments -n k8squest`

READY ustuniga qarang. Qancha stable pod bor? Qancha canary pod?

Service endpoint larini tekshiring:
`kubectl get endpoints app-service -n k8squest`

IP larni saning — bu trafik qabul qilayotgan BARCHA pod larni ko'rsatadi (ham stable, ham canary).

Trafik taqsimotini test qiling:
`for i in $(seq 1 20); do kubectl run -it --rm test-$i --image=busybox --restart=Never -n k8squest -- wget -q -O- app-service; done`

"Stable" va "Canary" necha marta ko'rinishini saning. 50/50 ga yaqinmi? Canary uchun bu noto'g'ri!

💡 Yechimga yaqin maslahat:

90/10 taqsimotga erishish uchun replika sonlarini to'g'rilang.

Qadamlar:
1. Stable ni 9 replikaga scale qiling:
   `kubectl scale deployment app-stable --replicas=9 -n k8squest`

2. Canary ni 1 replikaga scale qiling:
   `kubectl scale deployment app-canary --replicas=1 -n k8squest`

3. Pod lar tayyor bo'lishini kuting:
   `kubectl get deployments -n k8squest -w`

4. Taqsimotni tekshiring:
   ```bash
   STABLE=$(kubectl get deployment app-stable -n k8squest -o jsonpath='{.status.readyReplicas}')
   CANARY=$(kubectl get deployment app-canary -n k8squest -o jsonpath='{.status.readyReplicas}')
   echo "Stable: $STABLE, Canary: $CANARY"
   echo "Canary foizi: $((CANARY * 100 / (STABLE + CANARY)))%"
   ```

Taxminan 10% canary ko'rsatishi kerak!

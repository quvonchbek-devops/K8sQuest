Joriy resurs turini tekshiring:
`kubectl get deployments,statefulsets -n k8squest`

Bu Deployment mi yoki StatefulSet?

Pod nomlarini tekshiring:
`kubectl get pods -n k8squest -l app=database`

Deployment pod lari tasodifiy nomlarga ega: database-7d8f9c-abc12
StatefulSet pod lari barqaror nomlarga ega: database-0, database-1, database-2

Stateful ilovalar (ma'lumotlar bazalari, xabar navbatlari) uchun barqaror identifikatsiya kerak!

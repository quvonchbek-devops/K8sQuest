💡 Yechimga yaqin maslahat:

Deployment ni StatefulSet ga aylantiring.

Qadamlar:
1. Mavjud Deployment ni o'chiring:
   `kubectl delete deployment database -n k8squest`

2. O'rniga StatefulSet yarating:
   `kubectl apply -f solution.yaml`

3. Pod lar tartib bilan ishga tushishini kuzating (0, keyin 1, keyin 2):
   `kubectl get pods -n k8squest -l app=database -w`

4. Barqaror nomlarni tekshiring:
   `kubectl get pods -n k8squest -l app=database`

Ko'rinishi kerak: database-0, database-1, database-2 (tasodifiy hash emas)!

🔍 Kuzatuv maslahat:

Qanday resurslar mavjudligini tekshiring:
`kubectl get deployments,replicasets -n k8squest`

Deployment siz mustaqil ReplicaSet (web-app-rs) bormi?

Image ni yangilashga urining:
`kubectl set image replicaset/web-app-rs web=hashicorp/http-echo:latest -n k8squest`

Pod larni tekshiring:
`kubectl get pods -n k8squest -l app=webapp`

Yangilanish ishladimi? Javob: ReplicaSet spec yangilandi, lekin MAVJUD pod lar o'zgarmadi!

ReplicaSet lar rolling update qilmaydi — pod larni qo'lda o'chirishingiz yoki yangi ReplicaSet yaratishingiz kerak bo'ladi.

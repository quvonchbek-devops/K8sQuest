💡 Yechimga yaqin maslahat:

ReplicaSet ni Deployment ga aylantiring.

Qadamlar:
1. Mustaqil ReplicaSet ni o'chiring:
   `kubectl delete replicaset web-app-rs -n k8squest`

2. O'rniga Deployment yarating:
   `kubectl apply -f solution.yaml`

3. Tekshiring:
   `kubectl get deployments,replicasets,pods -n k8squest`

Ko'rishingiz kerak:
- 1 ta Deployment
- 1 ta ReplicaSet (deployment TOMONIDAN yaratilgan)
- 3 ta Pod

Deployment avtomatik ReplicaSet ni yaratdi va boshqarmoqda!

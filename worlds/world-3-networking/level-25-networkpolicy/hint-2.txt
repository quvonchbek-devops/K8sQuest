🧭 Yo'naltiruvchi maslahat:

NetworkPolicy da NOTO'G'RI label selector bor.

**Frontend Pod:** `app: frontend` label ga ega
**NetworkPolicy:** faqat `app: admin-tool` label li pod lardan trafikka ruxsat beradi

```yaml
ingress:
- from:
  - podSelector:
      matchLabels:
        app: admin-tool    # ❌ Noto'g'ri label
```

`frontend` ≠ `admin-tool` → Frontend dan barcha trafik RAD ETILADI

Tuzatish: NetworkPolicy ni `app: admin-tool` o'rniga `app: frontend` label li pod lardan trafikka ruxsat beradigan qilib o'zgartiring.

```yaml
# OLDIN (buzilgan):
app: admin-tool    # Noto'g'ri

# KEYIN (tuzatilgan):
app: frontend      # To'g'ri
```

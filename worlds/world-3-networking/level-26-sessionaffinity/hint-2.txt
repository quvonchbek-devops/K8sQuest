Service da session affinity konfiguratsiyasi yetishmayapti. Usiz har bir so'rov IXTIYORIY pod ga taqsimlanadi.

Tuzatish: Service spec ga `sessionAffinity: ClientIP` qo'shing:

```yaml
spec:
  sessionAffinity: ClientIP
  sessionAffinityConfig:
    clientIP:
      timeoutSeconds: 10800  # 3 soat
```

Bu qanday ishlaydi:
1. Klient birinchi so'rovda Pod 2 ga tushadi
2. Service eslab qoladi: "bu IP → Pod 2"
3. Keyingi barcha so'rovlar Pod 2 ga ketadi
4. Mapping timeoutSeconds dan keyin tugaydi

Pod larda readiness probe lar YETISHMAYAPTI. Ularsiz Kubernetes pod larni service endpoint lariga darhol qo'shadi, hatto ilova trafik xizmat qilishga tayyor bo'lmasa ham.

Readiness probe siz: Pod ishga tushdi → darhol endpoint larga qo'shildi → trafik keldi → ERROR!
Readiness probe bilan: Pod ishga tushdi → probe kutadi → probe muvaffaqiyat → endpoint ga qo'shildi → trafik keldi → OK!

Tuzatish — ikkala pod ga readiness probe qo'shing:
```yaml
readinessProbe:
  httpGet:
    path: /
    port: 8080
  initialDelaySeconds: 5
  periodSeconds: 5
  failureThreshold: 1
```

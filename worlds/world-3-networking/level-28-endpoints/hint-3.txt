✅ Yechim maslahat:

broken.yaml da IKKALA pod ga (web-app-1 va web-app-2) readinessProbe qo'shing:

```yaml
    readinessProbe:
      httpGet:
        path: /
        port: 8080
      initialDelaySeconds: 5
      periodSeconds: 5
      failureThreshold: 1
```

Qadamlar:
1. broken.yaml ni tahrirlang — har ikkala pod ning container spec iga readinessProbe qo'shing
2. Eski pod larni o'chiring: `kubectl delete pod web-app-1 web-app-2 -n k8squest`
3. Apply qiling: `kubectl apply -f broken.yaml`
4. Pod lar tayyor bo'lishini kuzating: `kubectl get pods -n k8squest -l app=web -w`

0/1 → 1/1 o'tishi readiness probe muvaffaqiyat bo'lganda sodir bo'ladi!

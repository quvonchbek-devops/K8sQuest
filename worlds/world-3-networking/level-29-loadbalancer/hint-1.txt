Service ga nima bo'lganini tekshiring:
1. `kubectl get service web-service -n k8squest` — EXTERNAL-IP ustuniga qarang
2. `kubectl describe service web-service -n k8squest` — "LoadBalancer Ingress: <pending>"

Muammo: LoadBalancer service lari bulut provayderlaridan tashqi load balancer ta'minotini talab qiladi.
Lokal klasterlarda (kind, minikube, k3s) ular abadiy "Pending" da qoladi, chunki load balancer yaratadigan bulut provayder yo'q.

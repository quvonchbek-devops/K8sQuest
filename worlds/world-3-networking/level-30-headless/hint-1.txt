🔍 Kuzatuv maslahat:

StatefulSet pod lari bir-birini nom orqali topa olmayapti.

Tekshiring:
1. Pod lar: `kubectl get pods -n k8squest -l app=web-cluster` (web-0, web-1, web-2)
2. Service: `kubectl get service web-cluster -n k8squest` — CLUSTER-IP ga qarang
3. DNS test: debug pod da `nslookup web-0.web-cluster.k8squest.svc.cluster.local` sinang

Muammo: Oddiy ClusterIP service faqat `web-cluster` → tasodifiy pod DNS beradi.
StatefulSet larga har bir pod uchun alohida DNS kerak: web-0.web-cluster, web-1.web-cluster...
Buning uchun "headless service" (clusterIP: None) kerak!

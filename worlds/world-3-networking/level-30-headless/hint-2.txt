Headless service (clusterIP: None):
- web-0.web-cluster.ns.svc... → web-0 pod IP ✅
- web-1.web-cluster.ns.svc... → web-1 pod IP ✅
- web-2.web-cluster.ns.svc... → web-2 pod IP ✅

Tuzatish — service da `clusterIP: None` qo'ying:
```yaml
spec:
  clusterIP: None  # ✅ Headless qiladi
  selector:
    app: web-cluster
```

- Barqaror pod nomlari (web-0, web-1)
- Barqaror DNS nomlari (web-0.service-nomi)

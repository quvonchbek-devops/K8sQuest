🔍 Maslahat 1: Muammoni Kuzatish

PVC nima uchun Pending da qotib turganini tekshiramiz:

1️⃣ PVC holatini tekshiring:
   kubectl get pvc -n k8squest
   STATUS ustuniga qarang — nima ko'rsatmoqda?

2️⃣ PVC tafsilotlarini ko'ring:
   kubectl describe pvc app-storage-claim -n k8squest
   Qidiring: Status, Events xatolari, so'ralgan storage va storage class

3️⃣ PersistentVolume lar mavjudligini tekshiring:
   kubectl get pv
   PVC ni qondira oladigan PV bormi?

4️⃣ Pod holatini tekshiring:
   kubectl get pod database-pod -n k8squest
   kubectl describe pod database-pod -n k8squest
   Volume mount muammolari haqida xabarlarni qidiring.

Asosiy kuzatuvlar:
- PVC Pending (Bound emas)
- Pod ContainerCreating (volume kutmoqda)
- Pod PVC bog'lanmaguncha ishga tusholmaydi
- PVC mos PV siz bog'lana olmaydi

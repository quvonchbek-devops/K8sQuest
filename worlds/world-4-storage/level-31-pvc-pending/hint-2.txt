🧭 Maslahat 2: PV va PVC Bog'lanishini Tushunish

PVC PersistentVolume ga bog'lanishi uchun quyidagilar MOS KELISHI kerak:
- Storage hajmi (PV >= PVC)
- Access mode (ReadWriteOnce, ReadWriteMany va boshqalar)
- StorageClass (nomi aniq mos kelishi kerak)

Hozirgi holat:
```
PVC so'rovi:
  storage: 1Gi
  accessModes: ReadWriteOnce
  storageClassName: fast-storage    ← Bu StorageClass bormi?
```

Tekshiring:
```bash
kubectl get storageclass
```

Agar "fast-storage" yo'q bo'lsa — muammo shu!

Yechim: PVC dagi storageClassName ni klasterda mavjud StorageClass ga o'zgartiring
(ko'pincha "standard", "local-path" yoki "hostpath").

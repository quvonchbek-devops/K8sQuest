🔍 Maslahat 1: Muammoni Kuzatish

Pod ishlayapti lekin ilova ma'lumotlarni topa olmayapti.

Tekshiring:
1. Pod loglarini ko'ring: `kubectl logs data-app -n k8squest`
2. Volume mount yo'lini tekshiring:
   `kubectl get pod data-app -n k8squest -o yaml | grep -A5 volumeMounts`
3. Ilova qaysi yo'lda ma'lumot kutmoqda vs volume qayerga mount qilingan?

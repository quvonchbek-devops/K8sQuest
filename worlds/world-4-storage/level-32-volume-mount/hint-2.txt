🧭 Maslahat 2: Volume Mount larni Tushunish

Muammo: Volume NOTO'G'RI yo'lga mount qilingan.

Volume mount ikki qismdan iborat:
```yaml
volumes:           # Volume MANBA (nima ulash)
- name: data-vol
  persistentVolumeClaim:
    claimName: data-pvc

volumeMounts:      # Volume MANZIL (qayerga mount qilish)
- name: data-vol
  mountPath: /wrong/path    # ❌ Noto'g'ri yo'l
```

Ilova `/data` yo'lida fayllarni kutadi, lekin volume `/wrong/path` ga mount qilingan.
mountPath ni ilovaning kutgan yo'liga o'zgartiring.

🧭 Maslahat 2: volumeClaimTemplates ni Tushunish

StatefulSet lar har bir pod uchun alohida PVC yaratish uchun `volumeClaimTemplates` ishlatadi:

```yaml
spec:
  volumeClaimTemplates:
  - metadata:
      name: data
    spec:
      accessModes: ["ReadWriteOnce"]
      resources:
        requests:
          storage: 1Gi
```

Bu har bir pod uchun: data-pod-0, data-pod-1, data-pod-2 PVC yaratadi.

Muammo ehtimol: noto'g'ri template nomi, mos kelmaydigan volumeMount nomi,
yoki mavjud bo'lmagan StorageClass.

✅ Maslahat 3: To'liq Yechim

broken.yaml da quyidagilarni tekshiring:
1. `volumeClaimTemplates[].metadata.name` va `volumeMounts[].name` MOS KELISHI kerak
2. StorageClass klasterda mavjud bo'lishi kerak
3. accessModes to'g'ri bo'lishi kerak

Tuzatgandan keyin:
```bash
kubectl delete -f broken.yaml
kubectl apply -f broken.yaml
kubectl get pvc -n k8squest    # Har bir pod uchun PVC yaratilishi kerak
kubectl get pods -n k8squest   # Pod lar tartib bilan ishga tushishi kerak
```

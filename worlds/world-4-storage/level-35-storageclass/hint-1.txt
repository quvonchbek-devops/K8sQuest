🔍 Maslahat 1: Muammoni Kuzatish

PVC Pending da qotib turgan — StorageClass bilan bog'liq muammo.

Tekshiring:
1. `kubectl get pvc -n k8squest` — STATUS: Pending
2. `kubectl describe pvc -n k8squest` — Events dagi xato xabarini o'qing
3. `kubectl get storageclass` — mavjud StorageClass larni ko'ring

PVC qaysi StorageClass ni so'ramoqda? Bu StorageClass klasterda bormi?

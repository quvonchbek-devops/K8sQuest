✅ Maslahat 3: To'liq Yechim

PV ning reclaim policy sini Retain ga o'zgartiring:

```yaml
persistentVolumeReclaimPolicy: Delete    # ❌ Xavfli
persistentVolumeReclaimPolicy: Retain    # ✅ Xavfsiz
```

Yoki mavjud PV ni patch qiling:
```bash
kubectl patch pv <pv-nomi> -p '{"spec":{"persistentVolumeReclaimPolicy":"Retain"}}'
```

`kubectl get pv` — RECLAIM POLICY: Retain bo'lishi kerak

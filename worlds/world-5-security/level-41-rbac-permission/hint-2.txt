🧭 Maslahat 2: RBAC ni Tushunish

Muammo: Role va RoleBinding yetishmayapti!

RBAC komponentlari:
- **ServiceAccount** — Pod ning identifikatsiyasi (kim?)
- **Role** — qanday ruxsatlar bor (nima qilishi mumkin?)
- **RoleBinding** — ServiceAccount ni Role ga bog'laydi (kimga nima ruxsat?)

```
ServiceAccount ←── RoleBinding ──→ Role
   (kim)           (bog'lovchi)    (ruxsatlar)
```

Pod da ServiceAccount bor, lekin Role va RoleBinding yaratilmagan!

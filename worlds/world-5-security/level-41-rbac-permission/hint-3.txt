✅ Maslahat 3: To'liq Yechim

Role va RoleBinding yarating:

```yaml
apiVersion: rbac.authorization.k8s.io/v1
kind: Role
metadata:
  name: pod-reader
  namespace: k8squest
rules:
- apiGroups: [""]
  resources: ["pods"]
  verbs: ["get", "list", "watch"]
---
apiVersion: rbac.authorization.k8s.io/v1
kind: RoleBinding
metadata:
  name: pod-reader-binding
  namespace: k8squest
subjects:
- kind: ServiceAccount
  name: app-sa          # Pod ning ServiceAccount nomi
  namespace: k8squest
roleRef:
  kind: Role
  name: pod-reader
  apiGroup: rbac.authorization.k8s.io
```

`kubectl apply -f solution.yaml`
`kubectl logs <pod-nomi> -n k8squest` — "Forbidden" yo'qolishi kerak

🔍 Maslahat 1: Kuzatish va Tahlil

Pod bilan nima bo'layotganini tekshiring:

1. Pod holatini ko'ring:
   ```bash
   kubectl get pod secure-app -n k8squest
   kubectl describe pod secure-app -n k8squest
   ```

2. Events va xato xabarlarini o'qing
3. SecurityContext konfiguratsiyasini tekshiring:
   ```bash
   kubectl get pod secure-app -n k8squest -o yaml | grep -A 15 securityContext
   ```

Qidiring: readOnlyRootFilesystem, runAsNonRoot ni, allowPrivilegeEscalation ni
Pod SecurityContext juda cheklangan bo'lishi mumkin.

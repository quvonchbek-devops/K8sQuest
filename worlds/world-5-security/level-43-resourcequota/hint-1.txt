🔍 Maslahat 1: Kuzatish va Tahlil

Namespace dagi ResourceQuota muammosini tekshiring:

1. Pod holatini ko'ring:
   ```bash
   kubectl get pods -n k8squest
   kubectl describe pod -n k8squest
   ```

2. ResourceQuota ni tekshiring:
   ```bash
   kubectl get resourcequota -n k8squest
   kubectl describe resourcequota -n k8squest
   ```

3. Used va Hard qiymatlarini solishtiring — limit oshib ketganmi?

Pod yaratishga uringaningizda "exceeded quota" xatosi chiqqan bo'lishi mumkin.

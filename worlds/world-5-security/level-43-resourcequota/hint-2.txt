🧭 Maslahat 2: ResourceQuota ni Tushunish

ResourceQuota namespace uchun resurs iste'moli limitlarini belgilaydi:
```yaml
spec:
  hard:
    requests.cpu: "2"        # Jami CPU so'rovlar limiti
    requests.memory: "2Gi"   # Jami xotira so'rovlar limiti
    limits.cpu: "4"          # Jami CPU limit lari limiti
    limits.memory: "4Gi"     # Jami xotira limit lari limiti
    pods: "10"               # Maksimal pod soni
```

Muammo: Pod ning resource request lari namespace quota dan oshib ketmoqda.
Masalan: Quota 2Gi xotira beradi, lekin pod 4Gi so'ramoqda.

Yechim: Pod ning requests/limits ni quota ga sig'adigan qilib kamaytiring.

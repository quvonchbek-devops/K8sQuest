🧭 Maslahat 2: NetworkPolicy ni ni Tushunish

NetworkPolicy ni pod lar orasidagi trafikni nazorat qiladi:

```yaml
apiVersion: networking.k8s.io/v1
kind: NetworkPolicy ni
metadata:
  name: backend-policy
spec:
  podSelector:          # Kimga tegishli (target)
    matchLabels:
      app: backend
  policyTypes:
  - Ingress            # Kiruvchi trafik qoidalari
  - Egress             # Chiquvchi trafik qoidalari
  ingress:
  - from:              # Kimdan ruxsat
    - podSelector:
        matchLabels:
          app: frontend
    ports:
    - port: 8080
```

Muammo: deny-all policy qo'yilgan, lekin kerakli trafikka ruxsat berilmagan.
Yechim: Aniq ingress/egress qoidalari bilan kerakli trafikka ruxsat bering.

✅ Maslahat 3: To'liq Yechim

deny-all policy ni aniq ruxsat qoidalari bilan almashtiring:

```yaml
apiVersion: networking.k8s.io/v1
kind: NetworkPolicy
metadata:
  name: allow-frontend-to-backend
  namespace: k8squest
spec:
  podSelector:
    matchLabels:
      app: backend
  ingress:
  - from:
    - podSelector:
        matchLabels:
          app: frontend
    ports:
    - port: 8080
      protocol: TCP
```

`kubectl delete networkpolicy --all -n k8squest`
`kubectl apply -f solution.yaml`
Pod lar orasidagi aloqani qayta test qiling.

🧭 Maslahat 2: Taint lar va Toleration lar

Taint lar pod larni node lardan qaytaradi (agar pod tolerate qilmasa):
```bash
# Node ga taint qo'shish
kubectl taint nodes <node> key=value:NoSchedule
```

Pod ga toleration qo'shish:
```yaml
tolerations:
- key: "key"
  operator: "Equal"
  value: "value"
  effect: "NoSchedule"
```

Muammo: Node da taint bor, pod da mos toleration yo'q.

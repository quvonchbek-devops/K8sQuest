🔍 Maslahat 1: PriorityClass Muammosi

Muhim pod lar past prioritet tufayli chiqarib yuborilmoqda (evict).

```bash
kubectl get priorityclass
kubectl get pods -n k8squest -o wide
kubectl describe pod -n k8squest    # Preempted xabarlarini qidiring
```

Barcha pod lar bir xil prioritetga ega — muhim ilovalar ajratilmagan!

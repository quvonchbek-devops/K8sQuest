🧭 Maslahat 2: PriorityClass ni Tushunish

PriorityClass pod larga ustunlik beradi — resurs tanqisligi vaqtida
past prioritetli pod lar chiqariladi (evict), yuqori prioritetlilar qoladi.

```yaml
apiVersion: scheduling.k8s.io/v1
kind: PriorityClass
metadata:
  name: critical-priority
value: 1000000
globalDefault: false
description: "Muhim ilovalar uchun"
```

Pod ga tayinlash:
```yaml
spec:
  priorityClassName: critical-priority
```
